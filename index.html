Welcome to Hotel Miraz

The Hotel Miraz is a modern, elegant 5-star and hotel overlooking the sea, perfect for a romantic, charming vacation, in the enchanting settings of sky.

The rooms at The Hotel Miraz are new, well-lit and inviting. Our reception staff will be happy to help you during your stay in Bangalore, suggesting itineraries, guided visits and some good restaurants in the historic centre. At your disposal in your room, fast Wi-Fi ideal for smart working. Sky TV with all the latest releases from the big screen and Sport. Innovative air conditioning system with primary air exchange to prevent the spread of viruses such as Covid-19.

While you enjoy a cocktail by the swimming pool on the rooftop terrace, you will be stunned by the breath taking view of clouds and the sky. Here, during your summer stays, our bar serves traditional Sicilian dishes, snacks and salads.
